What We Offer

Our Engineering Services

From initial concept to final construction, we provide comprehensive engineering solutions tailored to your specific project needs.

Highway & Road Design

Alignment Optimization

Advanced geometric design optimizing horizontal and vertical alignments for safety, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.

Pavement Design

Flexible and rigid pavement solutions using latest design standards and materials for long-lasting performance.

Junction Modelling

Signalized and unsignalized junction design with micro-simulation modeling for optimal traffic flow.

Traffic Assessments

Comprehensive traffic impact studies and transport assessments for development projects.

Bridge Engineering

Bridge Inspection

Detailed visual and non-destructive testing to assess structural condition and identify defects.

Load Rating

Assessment of bridge load-carrying capacity to current standards and vehicle loadings.

Refurbishment

Strengthening, repair, and rehabilitation designs to extend bridge service life.

New-Build Design

Complete design of pedestrian, highway, and railway bridges using advanced analysis tools.

Drainage & Flood Mitigation

Sustainable Drainage (SuDS)

Permeable pavements, rain gardens, and swales to manage runoff naturally and improve water quality.

Attenuation Solutions

Pond, tank, and basin design to store and release stormwater at controlled rates.

Hydraulic Modelling

1D/2D flood modeling using industry-standard software to predict and mitigate flood risk.

Pumping Stations

Design of pumping stations for flood defense and surface water management.

Geotechnical Solutions

Retaining Walls

Gravity, cantilever, and anchored wall designs for earth retention in challenging sites.

Soil Nailing

In-situ reinforcement of steep slopes and excavations for permanent and temporary works.

Slope Stability

Advanced analysis and stabilization design for natural and cut slopes.

Foundation Design

Shallow and deep foundation solutions tailored to ground conditions and structural loads.

BIM & Digital Engineering

3D Modelling

Intelligent 3D models with embedded data for design, visualization, and coordination.

Clash Detection

Automated clash detection and resolution across all disciplines before construction.

4D/5D BIM

Time (4D) and cost (5D) integration for enhanced project planning and control.

Digital Twins

Real-time digital replicas for asset management and lifecycle monitoring.

Structural Assessment

Structural Analysis

Detailed analysis of existing structures using advanced finite element methods.

Condition Surveys

Systematic inspection and reporting of structural defects and deterioration.

Remediation Strategies

Cost-effective repair and strengthening solutions for deficient structures.

Structural Monitoring

Installation and analysis of monitoring systems for critical structures.
